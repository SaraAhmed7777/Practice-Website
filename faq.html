Frequently Asked Questions

What is CUNYServe?

CUNYServe is a student-powered civic engagement platform that connects students across all 25 CUNY campuses with volunteer opportunities, projects, events, and leadership roles.

How do I find volunteering events?

Visit our Events page to explore upcoming one-time or dated volunteering events. You can filter by campus, format, and type to find the best fit.

What’s the difference between an event and a project?

Events are one-time or scheduled activities. Projects are long-term or recurring opportunities listed on our Find a Volunteer Project page under Get Involved.

Where can pre-med or health students find service opportunities?

Our Pre-Med & Health Pathways page highlights clinical volunteering, hospital partners, and events tailored to students pursuing healthcare careers.

Can I submit my own volunteer opportunity?

Absolutely! Reach out to us via the Contact page and tell us about your opportunity. We’ll review it and add it to the site if it fits our mission.

How can I become a team leader?

Check the Become a Team Leader section under Get Involved for more info about what team leaders do and how to sign up.

How can I support this initiative?

You can help by spreading the word, becoming a team leader, or donating to trusted organizations via our Donate Now page.

Where can I read student stories or submit my own?

Head to the bottom of this page or our FAQ page to submit your civic story. Sharing your experience helps inspire others to get involved.
