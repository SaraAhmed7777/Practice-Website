About the Website

Learn what inspired CUNYServe, how it connects students to civic engagement, and why your voice matters in shaping NYC's future.

What is CUNYServe?

CUNYServe is a platform created by students to inspire action, service, and leadership across the CUNY system. It was built with a simple mission: make it easier for students to engage in meaningful civic and volunteer work that benefits New York City's communities.

Why Civic Engagement?

Civic engagement is more than just volunteering — it's about being an active participant in shaping a better society. Whether you’re helping your neighborhood, supporting a cause you care about, or learning how institutions work, civic action builds stronger communities and deeper understanding. CUNYServe helps students become change-makers, one opportunity at a time.

What We Offer

  • A growing hub of real volunteer events and long-term projects available to CUNY students and NYC residents.
  • Clear distinction between Events (one-time, short-term opportunities) and Projects (recurring or ongoing commitments that build deeper skills and impact).
  • Specialized pages for different interest areas, including healthcare pathways for pre-med and nursing students.
  • A place to explore, lead, and grow through service—whether you want to help out once or make a semester-long impact.

Student-Built, Community-Driven

CUNYServe is a student-led civic initiative born out of passion for community service, digital access, and youth empowerment. We believe students are not just learners, but leaders. Our goal is to make community engagement more visible, approachable, and inclusive across all CUNY campuses and neighborhoods.

Get Involved Today

You don’t have to wait to make a difference. Whether you sign up for a one-time cleanup or commit to tutoring weekly, your time and energy matter. Explore our platform and be part of something bigger.
