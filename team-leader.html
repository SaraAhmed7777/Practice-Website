Become a Team Leader

Lead change. Inspire others. Make your mark through service.

Why Become a Team Leader?

Team Leaders are the heart of CUNYServe. You’ll organize volunteer projects, rally students, and bring real change to your community — while gaining leadership skills that last a lifetime.

🎓 Build Your Resume

Gain experience in leadership, planning, and outreach — all valuable for future careers.

🤝 Inspire Your Peers

Motivate other students to get involved and make service a part of CUNY culture.

🌎 Create Lasting Impact

Lead meaningful projects that directly help NYC communities in need.

How to Get Started

  1. 1. Fill out a short interest form (linked below).
  2. 2. Attend a 30-minute virtual training session.
  3. 3. Propose your own volunteer idea or choose one from our list.
  4. 4. Lead your project with support from the CUNYServe team.

Ready to Lead?

Fill out our interest form and a team member will reach out to guide you through the next steps.
